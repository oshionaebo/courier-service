Helpful Frequently Asked Questions

01 when can i expect my delivery

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen. Generally, our deliveries arrive within 3-5 business days from the date of shipment.

02 What is the first possible pick-up date

The first possible pick-up date is the next business day after you place your order. Our team will contact you to confirm a specific pick-up time and date.

03 How can i pay for the service

Our payment options include credit cards (all major brands), debit cards, and net banking. We also offer financing options and payment plans for large orders.
